Scaling with multiple GPUs on summitdev
Using PGI 17.4

2 MPI/GPU:  1.970 zones / usec
4 MPI/GPU:  3.239 zones / usec


128**3 (8 boxes)

2 MPI/GPU:  3.690 zones / usec
